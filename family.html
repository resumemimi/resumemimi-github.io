MY FAMILY



"Families are the compass that guided us. They are the inspiration to reach great heights, and our comfort when we occasinally falter."

Brad Henry


This is my family. There are five members in my family including me. I am the first daughter in my family. My father name is Abdul Hafidz, he is a retired police officer. As our father, he allows us to make mistake as he realizes that making mistake is part of growing up. However, he makes it clear that repeated the same mistakes won't be tolerated. My mother name is Mai Marimah, she is a draughtsman. For me and my siblings, my mother can be describe as supportive and lovely mother. She always there beside us whenever we have problems. She always make sure that we know that she really love us. Without my parents, I am nothing. Other than that, I have a younger brother and sister. Both of them are also a precious person in my life.
